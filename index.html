Our Covenant

As members of the young adult group at Countryside Church, we commit to communicating with love, openness, and kindness, always assuming good intentions. We strive to provide thoughtful feedback and to express our feelings when hurt, while being open to listening when we have hurt others. Radical inclusivity is at the heart of our group, where everyone, regardless of their level of commitment, is warmly welcomed. We pledge to respect each other, the physical spaces we use, and any borrowed items. Confidentiality is crucial to the safety of our community, and we will protect the trust placed in us. We acknowledge that this covenant is a living document, open to revision as we grow together. Our purpose is to make a positive impact on the world and in our communities, and to serve the spiritual, practical, and communal needs of those navigating the early stages of adulthood.
